Who I am

Playful by nature.
Precise by practice.

Hey! I'm Anshu — a multimedia designer at UT Austin double-dipping in a B.F.A in Design and a B.S. in Arts & Entertainment Technology, because apparently one degree wasn't chaotic enough.

I'm passionate about interactive design and finding ways to make digital experiences feel genuinely fun and human. Whether it's a brand, an app, a game, or something in between — I care about the little moments that make someone go "wait, that's really cool."

Currently looking for internships & opportunities where I can make cool stuff with cool people. Open to all the things!
